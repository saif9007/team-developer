Farming Guidance

Season wise step-by-step guide

Wheat – Rabi Season Timeline

1

Land Preparation (Oct)

Deep ploughing, leveling, add FYM/compost, remove weeds.

2

Sowing (Nov)

Use certified seeds, proper spacing, and recommended seed rate.

3

Irrigation & Fertilizer

First irrigation 20–25 days after sowing. Split nitrogen doses.

4

Weed & Pest Management

Use recommended weedicides, keep field clean and monitor pests.

5

Harvesting (March–April)

Harvest when grains are hard and golden yellow, avoid delay.

Seasonal Tips

  • Check weather before irrigation and pesticide spray.
  • Use mixed cropping to reduce risk.
  • Store grains in clean, dry, and pest-free godowns.
