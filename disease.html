Common Crop Diseases

Yellow Rust – Wheat (गेहूं)

Symptoms: Yellow stripes / spots on leaves, powdery rust.

Solution: Use rust-resistant varieties, avoid late sowing, and spray recommended fungicide after consulting local expert.

Blast – Rice (धान)

Symptoms: Spindle-shaped brown spots with grey center on leaves and neck.

Solution: Avoid excess urea, maintain drainage, and use recommended fungicide like Tricyclazole on advice.

Late Blight – Potato (आलू)

Symptoms: Dark brown blight on leaves, rapid rotting in cool, wet weather.

Solution: Avoid overhead irrigation in evening, remove infected leaves, use fungicide as per recommendation.
